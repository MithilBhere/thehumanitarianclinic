About the Founder : Dr. Satish S. Savant

Dr. Satish S. Savant is the esteemed founder and director of the Humanitarian Clinic: Skin, Hair, and Laser Centre in Mumbai, established in 1989. A pioneering force in dermatosurgery in India, Dr. Savant has been recognized for his groundbreaking contributions and excellence in dermatology and cosmetic surgery.

Founding Achievements

  • Founded the Humanitarian Clinic in 1989, specializing in skin, hair, and laser treatments.
  • Pioneered dermatosurgery practices in India, contributing extensively to the field.
  • Authored the “Textbook of Dermatosurgery and Cosmetology: Principles and Practice” (3rd Edition).

Specialty

Expertise in Dermatosurgery and Cosmetology, with a focus on advancing medical and surgical dermatology in India.

Experience

Over 30 years of experience in dermatology, with numerous national and international publications, workshops, and contributions to dermatological research and education.

Awards and Achievements

  • Recipient of Ambadi Oration, Dr. P.N. Behl Oration, and Dr. Marqueaes Oration.
  • Honored with Dr. Bishnupriyadebi National Award for Best Research Paper and several other prestigious awards.
  • Received the Shri Vasantrao Naik Foundation State Award and Government of Maharashtra Felicitation.
