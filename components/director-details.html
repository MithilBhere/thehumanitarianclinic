About the Chief Consultant: Dr. Sushil S. Savant

Dr. Sushil S. Savant serves as the chief consultant dermatosurgeon at The Humanitarian Clinic: Skin, Hair, and Laser Centre in Mumbai, India. With a distinguished academic record, he earned the top position with a gold medal at his post-graduate M.D. examination, achieving a notable score of 79.6% in the year 2017.

Leadership Achievements

  • Awarded the Dr. C.K. Jagavkar Award in 2019 for his presentation on dermatosurgical approaches to pre-malignant and malignant skin lesions, covering a study of 32 cases.
  • Recipient of the ACS(I) Dr. N. Umashankar Best Video Presentation Award for his work on dermal fat grafting in Perry Romberg Syndrome, 2023.

Publications and Contributions

Dr. Savant has made significant contributions to the field of dermatosurgery and cosmetology. He served as the principal author for three chapters and co-author for four chapters in the 3rd edition of the “Textbook of Dermatosurgery and Cosmetology: Principles and Practice.”

Publications and Presentations

With 45 publications as a principal author in indexed journals and 63 presentations as a speaker and faculty at various conferences, Dr. Savant continues to share his expertise and knowledge within the dermatology and surgical community.

Awards and Recognitions

  • Dr. C.K. Jagavkar Award for Dermatosurgical Study on Skin Lesions (2019)
  • ACS(I) Dr. N. Umashankar Best Video Presentation Award (2023)
